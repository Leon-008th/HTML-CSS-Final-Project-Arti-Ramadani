Economy

Food

Food represents agricultural output and population sustenance. Every citizen consumes 1 Food per day, while military units consume 2 Food per day while stationed and 3 Food per day while moving or fighting. If Food reaches 0, starvation begins: population growth halts, happiness drops by –25%, and units lose 10% health per day until Food is restored.

Produced by

  • Farms
  • Fisher Huts
  • Hunting Lodges

Statistics

Cost40 wood
Workers2 Farmers
Base Output6 food/day
Bonus+25% on plains
UpgradesIrrigation (+3), Windmill (+15%)

Lumber

Lumber is used for early construction, weapons, tools, carts, and ships.

  • Harvested from Forests
  • Regenerates slowly
  • Overharvesting reduces yield
  • Roads increase speed by +40%
Cost50 food
Workers3 Woodcutters
Base Output8 wood/day
Bonus+30% dense forest
UpgradesSawmill (+5), Cart Route

Stone

Stone is required for castles, walls, and siege weapons.

  • Extracted from Stone Nodes
  • Slow transport
  • High maintenance
Cost60 lumber
Workers3 Miners
Base Output5 stone/day
Bonus+2 if near mountain
UpgradesReinforced Shaft (+20%)

Gold

Gold is used for diplomacy, research, and armies.

  • Trade
  • Taxes
  • Gold Mines
Cost40 lumber, 20 stone
Workers2 Traders
Base Output6 gold/day
Bonus+2 per city
UpgradesTrade Routes, Bank Vault
